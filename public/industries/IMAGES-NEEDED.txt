SAK Fasteners - Industry Images
=================================

Place your industry application images in this directory with the following filenames:

1. construction.jpg - Construction & Infrastructure projects
2. electrical.jpg - Electrical & Sanitary installations
3. furniture.jpg - Furniture & Interior applications
4. upvc.jpg - uPVC & Window Systems

Recommended specifications:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Dimensions: 1200x900 pixels (4:3 aspect ratio)
- File size: Under 500KB for optimal web performance
- Show fasteners in real-world application contexts

The Next.js Image component will automatically optimize these images for different screen sizes.
